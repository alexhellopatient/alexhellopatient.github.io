Communication Services.

Hello Patient deploys artificial intelligence and machine learning models including large language models (collectively “Models”) to communicate and share information with and obtain instructions and information from clients and other users associated with Customer (collectively “Clients”) using synthetic voices and voice recognition as well as text-based interfaces to simulate verbal and written conversations between Clients and service providers (the “Communication Services”). While the Communication Services are intended to automate interactions with Clients and reduce the need for human oversight the Communication Services also include interactions between human personnel engaged by Hello Patient (the “Human Operators”) when Hello Patient determines involvement of a Human Operator in a conversation between Hello Patient’s software platform (the “Platform”) and a Client is appropriate. The functionality available to Customer and its Clients as part of the Communication Services is further set forth in one or more Order Forms.
